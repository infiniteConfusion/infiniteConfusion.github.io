General Console Repairs

We handle all types of Consoles from the NES to the Switch, The OG Xbox to the Xbox series, PlayStation 1-4, and many more.
***Excludes PlayStation 5
From display complications, no power, or trouble reading the game, we have seen it All and can get your console up and running again.

All repairs Start at $45.00 and go up depending on level of severity and parts required. All parts will be provided at wholesale, NO upcharge, So you pay what we pay.

Console Cleanings

All consoles brought in for cleanings must Turn On, Display and read Games as intended, Otherwise it will be classified as a repair. Cleanings will include a full break down of the console, the shell scrubbed, the motherboard cleaned and fresh thermal paste applied. (If required)

Console Cleanings are set at $35.00 regardless of the console type.

Controllers/attachments

Controller repairs start at $30.00 and go up depending on parts (i.e. new analogsticks) Controller cleanings are $25.00, but same as Consoles, they must show to be functioning as intended before we start, otherwise it will be classified as a repair.
