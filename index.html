What We Do

Console Repairs & Cleaning

We handle repairs and cleaning for most consoles, including controller work. PS5 consoles receive cleaning services only, but we do repair PS5 controllers.

Mods & Custom Builds

We offer console mods, homebrew setups, and shell swaps. Specialized services include IPS screen upgrades for Game Boy, Game Boy Advance, and SP.

Guitars & Audio Gear

We also service guitars, basses, and other string instruments — from setups and repairs to full modifications. Plus, we handle amps, pedals, and other audio equipment.
